Overview

Welcome to the Med Minds BMI Prediction Research project. This website presents our comprehensive machine learning research on predicting Body Mass Index (BMI) using survey-based data from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) 2024 dataset.

Research Objective

Develop and evaluate machine learning models for BMI prediction using easily accessible survey features, enabling scalable obesity screening without requiring clinical visits.

Dataset

We utilize the BRFSS 2024 dataset with 414,633 valid samples, identifying 16 optimal predictors from 302 available variables through systematic feature selection.

Results

Our Stacking Ensemble achieves strong performance with R² = 0.8858, MAE = 2.26 BMI points, and 96.3% within-1-category accuracy, demonstrating the feasibility of survey-based obesity prediction at population scales.
